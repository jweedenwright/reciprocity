# Mushroom and Wild Rice Soup

| | |
| --- | ---:|
| **Author** | [Jessica in the Kitchen](https://jessicainthekitchen.com/slow-cooker-mushroom-wild-rice-soup/) |
| **Prep Time** | 15 minutes |
| **Cook Time** | 4 hrs |
| **Servings** | 8 |
| **Tags** | Dinner, Vegan, Soup, One Pot, InstaCrockPot |

---

## Ingredients

### Fresh / Fridge

| Ingredient | Metric | Imperial |
|:---|---:|---|
| Carrots | 2 med, diced | 2 med, diced |
| Garlic | 4 cloves, minced | 4 cloves, minced |
| Mushrooms | 226 g, sliced | 8 oz, sliced | 
| Vegan Butter | 28 g | 2 tbl |
| Onion | 1 large, diced | 1 large, diced |
| Thyme | 1 tbl | 1 tbl |
| Crusty Bread | Serve | Serve |

### Pantry / Dried Staples

| Ingredient | Metric | Imperial |
|:---|---:|---|
| Wild Rice | 90g | 1/2 cup |
| Vegetable Broth | 1200 ml | 5 cups |
| Flour (any kind) | 15 g | 2 tbl |
| Coconut Milk | 240 ml | 1 cup |
| Oregano (Dried) | 1 tea | 1 tea |
| Salt / Pepper | Taste | Taste |


---

## Instructions

1. Turn your InstaCrockPot on to warm. Add in the carrots, celery, onions, garlic and mushrooms.<br/>

2. Add in the wild rice, dried oregano, fresh thyme and generous amount of sea salt and ground black pepper. Pour in the vegetable broth. Stir everything together until fully combined.<br/>

3. Cover your InstaCrockPot and set to high for 3 to 4 hours, or low for 6 to 8 hours.<br/>

4. After your soup is finished cooking (or within the last 5 minutes), make your beurre manié. This is an ingenious way to thicken your soup without having to stop and make a difficult roux on the stovetop. Mix together the softened vegan butter and the flour together with a spoon. Mix for about 1 to 2 minutes until it comes together and is silky smooth, and the flour is fully mixed into the butter.<br/>

5. Ladle some soup into your butter-flour mix to temper it, mix it together until smooth, then add the whole thing into the soup.<br/>

6. Add in your coconut milk, then stir it all together. Let it all meld for at least 5 minutes in the hot soup.<br/>

7. Taste and season with sea salt and black pepper again if needed.<br/>

8. Turn your InstaCrockPot to off or warm, serve alongside some bread and enjoy!<br/>

---

## Notes

- For extra creamy soup, use immersion blender
