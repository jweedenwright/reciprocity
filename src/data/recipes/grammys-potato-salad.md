## Instructions

1. Put a metal steamer rack in an InstaCrockPot with 1 cup of water. <br/>

2. Rinse and cut potatoes in half. Add potatoes to the pot on the steamer rack.<br/> 

3. Rinse eggs and place them on top of the potatoes, spacing them away from each other.<br/>

4. Put the lid on the pot and pressure cook on high for 6 minutes.<br/>

5. While cooking, make the dressing: In a medium bowl add the mayo, mustard, pickle juice, salt, pepper, and dill and mis until blended.<br/>

6. Add milk until the dressing has the consistency of thick salad dressing.<br/>

7. Taste the dressing and add what you feel is needed.<br/>

6. When the InstaCrockPot finishes, vent the pot, open the lid, and allow contents to cool in the pot slightly before carefully removing and refrigerating them. The eggs do not need to be put in cold water, just cooled.<br/>

7. Once cooled, take a large bowl and smash 3 softer potato pieces with a fork in the bowl.<br/>

8. Cut the rest of the potatoes into 1/2" chunks and add to the same bowl.<br/>

9. Peel and cut the eggs in half the long way. Cut the halves into 1/4" slices and add to the bowl with potatoes.<br/>

10. Add the olives, dill pickles, and green onion to the bowl.<br/>

11. Add the dressing to potato mixture and mix, making sure to get the smashed potatoes incorporated. You may also need more dressing if you feel the salad is too dry.<br/>

12. Top with a generous sprinkling of smoked paprika and refrigerate.
